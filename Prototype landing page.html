Welcome to Build It All Intranet

This is where you can find all the information about your intranet system, its features, and how it benefits you.
This intranet was designed to replace your current manual system with a more efficient and user friendly solution.

Key Features:

Tailored for Admin and Support Staff:

The intranet is designed to simplify routine administrative tasks,
offering easier navigation and quicker access to important company resources.
As well as, helping employees and teams stay organised and work more efficiently.

Technical Specialists’ Hub:

A dedicated section for technical staff, providing streamlined access to technical
documentation, maintenance logs, and system updates.

To-Do List & Task Management:

Employees will be able to create, track, and manage their daily tasks
through an intuitive to-do list feature. For team leaders and managers, a dedicated
dashboard will allow for better visibility into project progress, helping to balance
workloads and ensure tasks are completed on time.

Manager’s Dashboard:

The dashboard will provide managers with a clear overview of team progress,
individual workloads, and overall project status. This feature will be key to
improving resource allocation, monitoring task completion, and balancing team efficiency.

Knowledge Management:

Employees can create and share knowledge through "Topics" and "Posts."
These can range from technical knowledge such as software configurations or solutions
to technical issues, to administrative guidance such as how to order office supplies

User Access and Security:

Access to the system will be secure, with employee registration limited to
internal staff via their company email addresses. Strong password protection will ensure
the security of the system, and we will enforce policies to prevent the creation of
duplicate or unauthorized accounts.
